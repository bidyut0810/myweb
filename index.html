About Goku's UI Omen State

When a male Saiyan enters this state, his hair is slightly spikier and more solid,with silver highlights and oftentimes having loose strands. His eyes take on a more stern, defined shape, sporting silver-colored irises and visible pupils (similar to Super Saiyan 3 and Super Saiyan God).
He also gains a complex silver, purple and blue aura consisting of rippling, fire-like energy, complete with sparkling particles traveling upwards much like Super Saiyan God before it. In the manga, the user also slims down in the same way Super Saiyan God does.
In a promotional art for Dragon Ball Super depicted the aura as red while the other in conjunction with blue.In the art, the auras are surrounded by a halo of energy.
